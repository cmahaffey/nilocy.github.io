Born out of the frustration of looking up new characters when I was learning to read Chinese, this project is the actualization of a longtime dream. Rather than relying on pin yin, the phoneticization of a character, or drawing a character, which relies on knowing the stroke order, Radicalize uses Chinese radicals and a simple point and click system to allow users to look up new characters.

Movies use music for a variety of reasons, and the songs in them can bring back special memories for us all. However, movie soundtracks are limited, and often ignore some of a movie's greatest music. Soundtrak is a solution. Pulling from IMDB's massive song database, Soundtrak provides a user all the songs in a movie, and gives the user a place to listen to them.

Soundtrak
Soundtrak
is a Rails app utilizing OMDB, the Open Movie Database, and Spotify APIs together with, a database populated via IMDB's extensive list of songs. Soundtrak is currently limited to movies beginning with "al", "fro", and "rush" due to heroku's database limitations.
